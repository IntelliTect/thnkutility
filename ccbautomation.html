Oracle’s Customer Care and Billing System, also known as CC&B, plays a critical role in the business operations of many utility companies. CC&B, while very powerful, can be costly and difficult to manage and maintain. However, given a few key newcomers to the software integration and testing side, you can now get tremendous gains and lower costs, here is how.

The Oracle Customer Care and Billing System, which has been in release for more than 10 years is an Enterprise platform for for utilities who have thousands to millions of customers. The application (seen here) is a rock solid solution for many customers across the globe.

Although its usefulness to a utility company in the power and energy sector can be tremendous, like most software comes with a few drawbacks. Those drawbacks are mainly ALM/DevOps and QA/Testing Automation. The lack of these toolsets can increase costs and time to maintain this key piece of software for your group or organization.

However, a release called CC&B Automation (CCBAutomation.com) allows for both a Test harness to write clear and straightforward automated tests that are easy to use and modify as well as ALM/DevOps.

This testing Harness and QA cycle toolset allows you to never have to worry about the right data being present regardless of the system you are testing against. Some additional benefits include:

  • Reduce Time for Creating Tests
  • Increase Test Reliability
  • Reduce the Expertise Required
  • Built on Industry Standard Tooling

In addition, the ALM or DevOps tools, allow for you to comply with auditing requirements, Track requirements, use Source Control and automate your deployment thousands and thousands of times. In addition, some other benefits you get are:

  • Continuous Integration
  • Promotion Environments
  • Trialing & Dry Runs
  • Backups
